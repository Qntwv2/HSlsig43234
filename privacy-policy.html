Privacy Policy

Vuohi.org is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services. By using Vuohi.org, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary for the operation of the application. This may include:

  • Personal information provided during registration (e.g., username).
  • Information related to your use of Vuohi.org (e.g., usage statistics, user-generated content).
  • Technical information like IP addresses, browser types, and operating systems, collected only during sessions for security and functionality purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, which includes:

  • Managing your account and personalizing your experience.
  • Enhancing the security and performance of Vuohi.org.
  • Communicating with you regarding updates or customer support inquiries.

3. Data Privacy and Security

GDPR Compliance: Vuohi.org is compliant with GDPR regulations, ensuring that the personal data you provide is stored and handled in accordance with European privacy laws.

Password Security: All user passwords are securely hashed and stored to protect against unauthorized access.

4. User Tracking and Cookies

Vuohi.org does not engage in user tracking beyond what is necessary for the application's functionality. We use cookies only for session management and system performance. You have the option to disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect your experience with our services.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may use third-party services (e.g., for payment processing or analytics) that have their own privacy policies. We ensure that these services comply with applicable data protection laws.

6. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

Vuohi.org cooperates with law enforcement agencies when required by law. However, due to our strict privacy policies, the information we provide may be limited in its usefulness to law enforcement agencies.

7. Your Rights

As a user of Vuohi.org, you have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Request a copy of the data we store about you.
  • Withdraw your consent to data collection at any time, which may affect your ability to use certain features of Vuohi.org.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us, and we will take appropriate steps to comply with your request.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Vuohi.org may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes within the application. Continued use of the service after such modifications constitutes your acknowledgment of and consent to the updated policy.
